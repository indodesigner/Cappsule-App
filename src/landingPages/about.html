We bring value to healthcare.

Cappsule (provided by Swasth Bharat Techno Private Limited) is a healthcare platform aimed at connecting local pharmacies.Cappsule allows customers to compare the price and availability of various medical and surgical items in their locality and get any item delivered to their doorsteps instantly.

Our Mission

We have started with a mission to bring transparency, convenience, and affordability in the healthcare sector. Healthcare expenditure cannot be deferred or avoided with about 75% of the healthcare expenditure in India coming from out-of-pocket. This means that the vast majority of Indians cannot afford healthcare, and many of them end up with either heavy debts or are forced to succumb to serious illness.
We look forward to providing seamless cost-effective healthcare to all by minimizing dependence on unwanted medicines and investigations.
